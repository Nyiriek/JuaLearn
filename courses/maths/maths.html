Mathematics

Master the principles of mathematics, from algebra to calculus.

This comprehensive course covers a wide range of mathematical concepts, including:

  • Algebra: Solving equations, inequalities, and systems of equations.
  • Geometry: Properties of shapes, angles, and geometric proofs.
  • Trigonometry: Trigonometric functions, identities, and solving trigonometric equations.
  • Calculus: Limits, derivatives, and integrals.

By the end of this course, you will:

  • Develop a solid understanding of fundamental mathematical principles.
  • Gain problem-solving skills applicable to various real-world scenarios.
  • Be able to analyze and interpret mathematical data.

Prerequisites:

  • Basic understanding of arithmetic operations.
  • Familiarity with algebraic concepts such as variables and equations.

Additional Resources:

  • Khan Academy: Free online tutorials and exercises covering a wide range of mathematical topics.
  • MIT OpenCourseWare: Access free course materials from MIT's mathematics curriculum.
